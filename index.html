Population Growth

The current population of Jamaica is 2,951,697 as of Sunday, October 6, 2019, based on Worldometers elaboration of the latest United Nations data.

  • Jamaica 2019 population is estimated at 2,948,279 people at mid year according to UN data.
  • Jamaica population is equivalent to 0.04% of the total world population.
  • Jamaica ranks number 138 in the list of countries (and dependencies) by population.
  • The population density in Jamaica is 272 per Km2 (705 people per mi2).
  • The total land area is 10,830 Km2 (4,181 sq. miles)
  • 55.2 % of the population is urban (1,627,119 people in 2019)
  • The median age in Jamaica is 29.4 years.

Jamaican Proverb

"Nuh romp wid mawga cow, a cudda bull mumma!"

English translation: Don’t play with a skinny cow it could be the bull’s mother.

Proverb Meaning: Don’t underestimate people who may seem weak and insignificant because you don’t know what kind of powerful and influential people they have in their corner.
